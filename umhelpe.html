How UMSOCIAL works?

UMSOCIAL presents a fully integrated and flexible investment platform that facilitates the replication of trading strategies and the provision of one's own strategies for replication by fellow traders.

This user-friendly and dependable solution features a transparent fee structure and comprehensive information on strategies and their histories, enhancing the copying process for a superior trading experience.

Upon logging into the UMSOCIAL web application with a MetaTrader ID, investors can navigate to the Copy section, where they can explore available strategies, compare their performance histories, details, and fees, allocate funds, and initiate copying with just two clicks.

All strategies can be easily discovered and copied from the "Leaderboard" page without any long-term commitments, allowing investors with a MetaTrader ID to invest in strategies based on their individual risk preferences.

 

While utilizing UMSOCIAL, you may come across the following terms :

Strategy : Aggregated actions of a trader made available for replication by investors under specific conditions.

Master/Trader : An experienced trader who shares their trading strategy for replication by others.

Follower/Investor : An individual who allocates a portion of their trading account funds to replicate a strategy under certain conditions (investing)

 

Why UMSOCIAL is good for INVESTOR?

UMSOCIAL is a great solution for users looking for sources of passive income :

  • Affordable : Start copying from only $1 and use any of their real accounts for copying trades. No special requirements for leverage, account type, balance, trading terminal, etc. 
  • Customizable : Choose the copying ratio according to your own risk appetite. So, even a beginner trader with moderate capital will copy orders and profit from them.
  • Transparent : View the trading orders of the copied strategy.
  • Controllable : Manage the copied trades in your MT4 Terminal, such as closing the orders, adding and changing the stop-loss, take-profit, order volume, and many more.

How UMSOCIAL works for INVESTORS?

As an investor, you gain the maximum profit from UMSOCIAL when you copy multiple strategies. Strategies can balance each other out and help you to diversify your market risks. To start on UMSOCIAL, go to the "Leaderboard".

The Leaderboard is the main workspace for an investor. Here you can view all the available strategies ranked by adjustable filters. Here are the steps you may take before investing in UMSOCIAL :

  • Filter : You can filter and sort the strategies by profit, win rate, active days, popularity, risk, or performance fee.
